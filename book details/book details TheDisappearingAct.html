The Disappearing Act

by Catherine Steadman

★★★★☆ 4.1

The Disappearing Act is a suspenseful thriller about a struggling actress in Hollywood who becomes entangled in a web of lies and deceit after a fellow actress mysteriously vanishes. As she tries to uncover the truth, she realizes that nothing is as it seems.

Catherine Steadman delivers a fast-paced and intriguing story that keeps readers guessing until the very end.

Release Date: June 8, 2021 Length: 320 pages
