Seven Years of Darkness

by You-jeong Jeong

★★★★☆ 4.0

Seven Years of Darkness is a chilling psychological thriller that explores the aftermath of a tragic incident at a remote reservoir. The story follows a young boy and his father as they grapple with the consequences of their actions and the secrets they keep.

You-jeong Jeong masterfully weaves a dark and suspenseful tale that delves into themes of guilt, revenge, and redemption.

Release Date: November 5, 2019 Length: 400 pages
