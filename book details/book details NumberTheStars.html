Number the Stars

by Lois Lowry

★★★★★ 4.7

Number the Stars is a historical fiction novel set during World War II. It tells the story of Annemarie Johansen, a young girl who helps her Jewish friend escape the Nazis in Denmark.

Lois Lowry's novel is a powerful and moving story about bravery, friendship, and the fight for freedom.

Release Date: April 24, 1989 Length: 137 pages
