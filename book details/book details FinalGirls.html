Final Girls

by Riley Sager

★★★★☆ 4.0

Final Girls is a psychological thriller that follows Quincy Carpenter, the sole survivor of a massacre, as she tries to move on with her life. When another "final girl" is found dead, Quincy is forced to confront her past and uncover the truth about what really happened.

Riley Sager delivers a suspenseful and twist-filled story that keeps readers guessing until the very end.

Release Date: July 11, 2017 Length: 352 pages
