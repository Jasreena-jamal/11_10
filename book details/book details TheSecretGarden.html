The Secret Garden

by Frances Hodgson Burnett

★★★★☆ 4.3

The Secret Garden is a classic children's novel about Mary Lennox, a young girl who discovers a hidden, neglected garden on her uncle's estate. As she brings the garden back to life, she transforms her own life and the lives of those around her.

Frances Hodgson Burnett's novel is a heartwarming story of growth, friendship, and healing.

Release Date: August 1911 Length: 331 pages
