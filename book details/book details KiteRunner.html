The Kite Runner

by Khaled Hosseini

★★★★☆ 4.35

The Kite Runner tells the unforgettable story of Amir, a young boy from a wealthy family in Kabul, and Hassan, the son of Amir’s father’s servant. Set against a backdrop of a divided Afghanistan on the brink of turmoil, the two boys share a deep bond, bound by friendship and the joy of kite flying. However, one tragic incident shatters their lives and drives them apart, leaving Amir haunted by guilt and regret.

Years later, now an adult in America, Amir is given a chance to confront his past and seek redemption. His journey back to Afghanistan—now scarred by war—unearths buried secrets and brings him face-to-face with the possibility of making things right.

Release Date: May 29, 2003 Length: 371 pages
