A Farewell to Arms

by Ernest Hemingway

★★★★☆ 4.2

A Farewell to Arms is a novel set during World War I. It tells the story of an American ambulance driver, Frederic Henry, and his love affair with a British nurse, Catherine Barkley, amidst the chaos of war.

Ernest Hemingway's novel is a poignant exploration of love, loss, and the brutality of war.

Release Date: September 27, 1929 Length: 332 pages
