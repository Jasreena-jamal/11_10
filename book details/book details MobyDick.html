Moby-Dick

by Herman Melville

★★★★☆ 4.1

Moby-Dick is a classic novel that tells the story of Captain Ahab's obsessive quest to hunt the white whale, Moby Dick. The book explores themes of revenge, fate, and the struggle between man and nature.

Herman Melville's novel is a masterpiece of American literature and a profound exploration of the human spirit.

Release Date: October 18, 1851 Length: 585 pages
