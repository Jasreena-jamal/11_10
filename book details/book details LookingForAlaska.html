Looking for Alaska

by John Green

★★★★☆ 4.1

Looking for Alaska is a coming-of-age novel that follows Miles "Pudge" Halter as he enrolls in a boarding school and meets the enigmatic Alaska Young. The story explores themes of love, loss, and the search for meaning in life.

John Green's debut novel is a poignant and thought-provoking read.

Release Date: March 3, 2005 Length: 221 pages
