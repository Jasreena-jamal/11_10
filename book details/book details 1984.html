1984

by George Orwell

★★★★★ 4.7

1984 is a dystopian novel that explores a totalitarian regime led by Big Brother. The story follows Winston Smith, a man who begins to question the oppressive government and its control over truth and freedom.

George Orwell's novel is a chilling and thought-provoking masterpiece that remains relevant today.

Release Date: June 8, 1949 Length: 328 pages
