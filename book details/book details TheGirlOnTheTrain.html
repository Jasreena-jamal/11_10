The Girl on the Train

by Paula Hawkins

★★★★☆ 4.1

The Girl on the Train is a psychological thriller that follows Rachel, a woman who becomes entangled in a missing person investigation after witnessing something suspicious during her daily train ride. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she uncovers shocking truths about herself and those around her.

Paula Hawkins delivers a suspenseful and twist-filled story that keeps readers guessing until the very end.

Release Date: January 13, 2015 Length: 336 pages
