The Alchemist

by Paulo Coelho

★★★☆☆ 3.92

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is an inspiring allegorical tale about following one’s dreams and finding personal purpose. The story follows Santiago, a young Andalusian shepherd who dreams of discovering a treasure located in the Egyptian pyramids. Compelled by his vision, Santiago embarks on a journey across deserts and foreign lands, encountering mystical guides, unexpected obstacles, and profound truths along the way.

Guided by the mysterious alchemist and inspired by those he meets, Santiago learns about the power of perseverance, the language of the universe, and the importance of following his "Personal Legend"—his unique path to fulfillment. Through trials and triumphs, he discovers that true wealth lies not in material riches but in self-discovery and understanding one's place in the world.

The Alchemist is a poetic and timeless fable that encourages readers to pursue their passions, embrace change, and recognize that the journey itself is often the true reward. It has become an enduring classic, celebrated for its uplifting message and universal appeal.

Release Date: January 1, 1993 Length: 182 pages
