The Housemaid's Secret

by Freida McFadden

★★★★☆ 4.3

The Housemaid's Secret is a gripping psychological thriller that follows the story of a housemaid who uncovers dark secrets about her employer's family. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she finds herself in a dangerous situation where trust and survival are at stake.

Freida McFadden weaves a suspenseful tale filled with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Release Date: February 20, 2023 Length: 336 pages
