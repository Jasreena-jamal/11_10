Ella Enchanted

by Gail Carson Levine

★★★★☆ 4.2

Ella Enchanted is a retelling of the Cinderella story with a twist. Ella is cursed with the "gift" of obedience, which forces her to obey any command. She embarks on a journey to break the curse and find her own happily ever after.

Gail Carson Levine's novel is a charming and empowering tale for readers of all ages.

Release Date: April 25, 1997 Length: 240 pages
