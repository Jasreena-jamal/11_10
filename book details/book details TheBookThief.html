The Book Thief

by Markus Zusak

★★★★★ 4.7

The Book Thief is a historical novel set in Nazi Germany. It tells the story of Liesel Meminger, a young girl who finds solace in stealing books and sharing them with others, while Death narrates the story.

Markus Zusak's novel is a powerful and moving tale of love, loss, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Release Date: March 14, 2006 Length: 552 pages
