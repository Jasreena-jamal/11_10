Coraline

by Neil Gaiman

★★★★★ 4.5

Coraline is a dark fantasy novel about a young girl who discovers a mysterious door in her new home. Behind it lies an alternate world that mirrors her own, but with sinister secrets and dangers.

Neil Gaiman's novel is a creepy and captivating tale that has become a modern classic.

Release Date: August 4, 2002 Length: 162 pages
