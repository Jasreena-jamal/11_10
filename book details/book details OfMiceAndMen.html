Of Mice and Men

by John Steinbeck

★★★★☆ 4.2

Of Mice and Men is a novella that tells the story of two displaced ranch workers, George and Lennie, during the Great Depression. The book explores themes of friendship, dreams, and the harsh realities of life.

John Steinbeck's novel is a poignant and unforgettable tale of hope and loss.

Release Date: February 6, 1937 Length: 107 pages
