The Night Circus

by Erin Morgenstern

★★★★☆ 4.01

The Night Circus is a spellbinding tale of magic, mystery, and romance, centered on a circus that appears only at night and vanishes without warning. Known as Le Cirque des Rêves, it captivates visitors with its astonishing, otherworldly tents, each one filled with wonders and illusions beyond imagination. But behind the scenes, two young illusionists, Celia and Marco, are bound in a fierce competition by their mentors, forced to pit their magical abilities against one another in a contest they neither fully understand nor control.

As the circus tours the world, the rivalry between Celia and Marco becomes increasingly intense, and what was once a competition turns into a deep, forbidden love that threatens the lives of everyone involved. With the fate of the circus and its performers hanging in the balance, Celia and Marco must make choices that could unravel their world.

Release Date: September 13, 2011 Length: 506 pages
