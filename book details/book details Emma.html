Emma

by Jane Austen

★★★★☆ 4.1

Emma is a classic novel that follows the life of Emma Woodhouse, a wealthy and self-assured young woman who enjoys matchmaking. However, her meddling often leads to unintended consequences, and she learns valuable lessons about love and self-awareness.

Jane Austen's novel is a witty and charming exploration of relationships and society.

Release Date: December 23, 1815 Length: 474 pages
