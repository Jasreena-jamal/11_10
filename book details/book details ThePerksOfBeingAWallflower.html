The Perks of Being a Wallflower

by Stephen Chbosky

★★★★★ 4.5

The Perks of Being a Wallflower is a coming-of-age story told through letters written by Charlie, a socially awkward teenager. The book explores themes of friendship, love, mental health, and self-discovery.

Stephen Chbosky's novel is a heartfelt and relatable story that resonates with readers of all ages.

Release Date: February 1, 1999 Length: 213 pages
