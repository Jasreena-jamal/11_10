Words Of Radiance Name

by Brandon Sanderson

★★★★☆ 4.76

Words of Radiance, the second book in Brandon Sanderson's The Stormlight Archive, continues the epic saga of Roshar, a world besieged by destructive storms and ancient conflicts. The story follows several key characters, each on their own journey to uncover Roshar’s forgotten history and fight against growing threats.

Kaladin, once a lowly slave and now the captain of the royal guard, struggles to protect his people and master his newfound powers, even as he grapples with questions of loyalty and justice. Meanwhile, Shallan Davar, a young scholar with a mysterious past, embarks on a dangerous mission to understand ancient magical secrets that could save her world. As Dalinar Kholin seeks to unite warring factions to confront the impending danger of the Voidbringers, alliances are tested, and hidden truths are revealed.

Richly layered with political intrigue, breathtaking magic, and profound character growth, Words of Radiance delves into themes of honor, redemption, and the strength to confront inner darkness. Sanderson's masterful world-building and complex characters make this book an unforgettable continuation of a landmark fantasy series, praised for its depth, adventure, and the powerful journey of its heroes.

Release Date: March 4, 2014 Length: 1087 pages
