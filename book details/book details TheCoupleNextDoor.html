The Couple Next Door

by Shari Lapena

★★★★☆ 4.1

The Couple Next Door is a fast-paced thriller that delves into the secrets and lies of a seemingly perfect couple. When their baby goes missing during a dinner party, the couple's lives unravel as dark truths come to light.

Shari Lapena crafts a gripping story filled with twists and turns, keeping readers guessing until the shocking conclusion.

Release Date: August 23, 2016 Length: 308 pages
