To Kill a Mockingbird

by Harper Lee

★★★★★ 4.8

To Kill a Mockingbird is a novel set in the American South during the 1930s. It tells the story of Scout Finch, her brother Jem, and their father Atticus, a lawyer who defends a black man falsely accused of a crime.

Harper Lee's novel is a powerful exploration of race, justice, and morality.

Release Date: July 11, 1960 Length: 281 pages
