The Things They Carried

by Tim O'Brien

★★★★☆ 4.3

The Things They Carried is a collection of linked short stories about a group of soldiers during the Vietnam War. Tim O'Brien explores themes of war, memory, and the emotional burdens carried by soldiers.

This book is a powerful and moving portrayal of the human experience during wartime.

Release Date: March 28, 1990 Length: 233 pages
