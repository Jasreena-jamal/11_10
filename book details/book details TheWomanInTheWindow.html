The Woman in the Window

by A.J. Finn

★★★★☆ 4.0

The Woman in the Window is a psychological thriller that follows Anna Fox, an agoraphobic woman who spends her days watching her neighbors from her window. When she witnesses what she believes to be a crime in the house across the street, her world begins to unravel as she questions her own sanity and the truth of what she saw.

The novel explores themes of isolation, trauma, and the reliability of perception, keeping readers on edge with its twists and turns. A.J. Finn crafts a gripping narrative that delves into the complexities of the human mind and the secrets people keep.

Release Date: January 2, 2018 Length: 427 pages
