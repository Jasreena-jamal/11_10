The Lightning Thief

by Rick Riordan

★★★★☆ 4.3

The Lightning Thief is the first book in the Percy Jackson series. It follows Percy, a 12-year-old demigod, as he discovers his true identity and embarks on a quest to prevent a war among the Greek gods.

Rick Riordan combines humor, adventure, and mythology in this beloved fantasy novel.

Release Date: June 28, 2005 Length: 377 pages
