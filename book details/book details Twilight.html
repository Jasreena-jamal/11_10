Twilight

by Stephenie Meyer

★★★★☆ 4.0

Twilight is the first book in the Twilight Saga. It tells the story of Bella Swan, a teenager who falls in love with Edward Cullen, a vampire. Their romance is complicated by the dangers of Edward's world and the arrival of other vampires.

Stephenie Meyer delivers a captivating love story that has become a cultural phenomenon.

Release Date: October 5, 2005 Length: 498 pages
