The Art of War

by Sun Tzu

★★★★★ 4.6

The Art of War is an ancient Chinese military treatise attributed to Sun Tzu. It is a timeless guide to strategy, leadership, and conflict resolution, offering insights that are still relevant in modern times.

This book is a must-read for anyone interested in strategy, business, or personal development.
