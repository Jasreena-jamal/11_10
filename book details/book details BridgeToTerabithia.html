Bridge to Terabithia

by Katherine Paterson

★★★★★ 4.6

Bridge to Terabithia is a story of friendship and imagination. Jess and Leslie create a magical kingdom called Terabithia in the woods, but tragedy strikes, changing Jess's life forever.

Katherine Paterson's novel is a touching and unforgettable story about love, loss, and resilience.
