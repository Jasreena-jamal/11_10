The Broken Girls

by Simone St. James

★★★★☆ 4.1

The Broken Girls is a chilling mystery that alternates between two timelines: 1950 and 2014. The story revolves around a boarding school for troubled girls and the secrets it holds. When a journalist investigates the murder of her sister, she uncovers dark truths about the school and its past.

Simone St. James masterfully blends suspense, history, and the supernatural in this haunting tale.

Release Date: March 20, 2018 Length: 336 pages
