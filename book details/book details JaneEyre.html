Jane Eyre

by Charlotte Brontë

★★★★★ 4.5

Jane Eyre is a classic novel that follows the life of its titular character, an orphaned girl who grows up to become a governess. The story explores themes of love, independence, and morality as Jane navigates her relationship with the enigmatic Mr. Rochester.

Charlotte Brontë's novel is a timeless tale of resilience and self-discovery.

Release Date: October 16, 1847 Length: 500 pages
