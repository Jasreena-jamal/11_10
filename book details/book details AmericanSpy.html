American Spy

by Lauren Wilkinson

★★★★☆ 4.2

American Spy is a thrilling and thought-provoking novel that blends espionage, family drama, and political intrigue. The story follows Marie Mitchell, an intelligence officer during the Cold War, as she navigates the challenges of being a Black woman in a male-dominated field while grappling with her own moral dilemmas.

Lauren Wilkinson delivers a powerful narrative that explores themes of identity, loyalty, and the complexities of love and duty.

Release Date: February 12, 2019 Length: 304 pages
